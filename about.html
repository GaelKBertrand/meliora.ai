About Us

We harness the power of technology to assist medical professionals in the early detection of Alzheimer's disease in patients. Our culture has been structured to understand and harness artificial intelligence to assist humans and offer better solutions.

Meliora is a medical software developed to assist in a rapid diagnosis of Alzheimer's disease. It uses neuroimaging technology and artificial intelligence to do a neurological assessment and detect neuropathological threats that predate severe Alzheimer's cases in patients.
