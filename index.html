Artificially Intelligent Software That Diagnoses Alzheimer's Disease Ten Times Faster Than Humans

Meliora uses Deep Learning and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) images of the brain to detect pathophysiological conditions that predate Alzheimer’s Disease very early before they become a severe case with a 98 % accuracy rate.

The Deep Learning model is heavily trained on clinically diagnosed Alzheimer's disease subjects and other clinically normal subjects from various, highly credible, medical approved databases.

Some of Our Key Features

Trained using MRI scans and high-quality data

The deep learning model used in the software is trained using high quality data from various internationally recognized Alzheimer’s disease care centers.

Scans brain MRI scans fast

The software is capable of scanning MRI Images quite quickly and saves medical professionals the effort of looking at MRI scans to make decisions.

High Accuracy

The software is capable of outperforming humans in deciding on a patient's condition with an accuracy of 98 %.

Output highly informative data

The software can generate high quality statistics within ten minutes that medical professionals can use to make more informed decisions about the patient's condition.
