Artificially Intelligent Software That Diagnose Alzheimer's Disease Ten Times Faster Than Humans

Meliora uses Deep Learning and Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) images of the brain to detect pathophysiological conditions that predate Alzheimer’s Disease very early before they become a severe case with a 98 % accuracy

The Deep Learning model is heavily trained on clinically diagnosed Alzheimer's disease subjects and other clinically normal subjects from various and highly credible clinical databases.
